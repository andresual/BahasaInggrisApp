Simple Future Tense

Simple future tense untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi terjadi dimasa depan, secara spontan atau terencana.

 

Kalimat

Rumus Simple Future Tense

Contoh

Positif 

(+)

S + will + bare infinitive
 S + be (am/is/are) going to + bare infinitive

You will win

They are going to come

Negatif 

(-)

S + will + not + bare infinitive
 S + be (am/is/are) + not + going to + bare infinitive

You won’t win

They aren’t going to come

Interogative 

(?)

Will + S + bare infinitive
 Be (am/is/are) + S + going to + bare infinitive?

Will you win

Are they going to come
