Simple Present Tense

 Simple Present adalah tenses (pola kalimat) yang digunakan untuk menceritakan waktu sekarang dalam bentuk sederhana.

 

Kalimat

Rumus Simple Present Tense

Contoh

Positif 

(+)

S+V-1

S+/-auxiliary (do/does) + Infinitive

She likes eating out

S+be (am/is/are)

The children are naughty

Negatif 

(-)

S + auxiliary (do/does) + not + bare infinitive

She doesn’t like eating out

S + be(am/is/are) + not

The children aren’t naughty

Interogative 

(?)

Do/Does + S + bare infinitive

Does she like eating out

Be(am/is/are) + S

Are the children naughty
